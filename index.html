什么是EMDR？What is EMDR?

EMDR（眼动脱敏与再加工）是一种心理治疗方法。简单来说，咨询师会引导你做一些眼球运动，同时回忆让你困扰的经历。这个过程可以帮助大脑重新处理这些记忆，让它们不再像以前那样痛苦。EMDR已经被世界卫生组织（WHO）等权威机构认可，是一种安全有效的治疗方式。 EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing) is a type of therapy. In simple terms, the therapist guides you through eye movements while you recall a difficult experience. This helps your brain reprocess those memories so they no longer feel as distressing. EMDR is recognised by the WHO and other leading health organisations as a safe and effective treatment.
